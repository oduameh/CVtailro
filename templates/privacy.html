Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

CVtailro helps you tailor resumes and prepare interview materials. This page explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Basic Google profile information (name, email, avatar) to sign you in.
  • The resume and job description you submit to generate tailored outputs.
  • Generated files and usage metadata needed to show your history and run the service.

How we use data

  • To generate your tailored resume, match report, and talking points.
  • To store results so you can download them later.
  • To keep the service reliable and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers required to run the app, such as hosting, storage, and AI processing.

Retention and deletion

We keep your results so you can access your history. You can request deletion through the contact page.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads on some pages. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing activity. For more information, see the Google advertising privacy policy.

Security

We use access controls and monitoring to protect your data. No system is 100 percent secure, so please avoid uploading sensitive information you do not want stored.
