# AI Personal Branding on LinkedIn: Aligning Profile and Resume

Your LinkedIn profile and resume should tell the same story. AI can help you align language, keywords, and achievements so recruiters see consistent signals. This guide shows how to use AI for personal branding while keeping the content accurate.

## 1) Align your headline with your resume summary

Use AI to generate a headline based on your resume summary:

"Create a LinkedIn headline from this resume summary: [summary]."

## 2) Use AI to refine your About section

Prompt:

"Rewrite this About section to align with a [role] job description and include these keywords: [list]."

## 3) Ensure experience is consistent

Your LinkedIn experience bullets should mirror your resume achievements and metrics.

## 4) Highlight proof points

Add portfolio links, projects, and certifications that reinforce your resume claims.

## 5) Final checklist

- Headline aligns with resume summary
- About section includes role keywords
- Experience metrics match the resume
