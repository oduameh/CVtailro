# AI Job Transition Roadmap: A 90-Day Plan for Career Change

AI tools can accelerate career transitions by identifying skill gaps, generating tailored resumes, and improving job search execution. This roadmap provides a 90-day plan to move from your current role to a new industry using AI responsibly.

## 1) Week 1-2: Define target roles and skill map

Use AI to analyze job descriptions for your target roles. Extract required skills and compare them to your current experience. This creates a clear skill map.

## 2) Week 3-5: Build proof with projects

Use AI to brainstorm small projects that demonstrate target skills. Build one or two portfolio projects and document outcomes.

## 3) Week 6-8: Resume and LinkedIn optimization

Use AI to tailor your resume summary and experience bullets. Align your LinkedIn profile with the same keywords.

## 4) Week 9-12: Job search execution

Apply consistently, track results, and use AI to refine follow-up messages and interview preparation.

## 5) Final checklist

- Skill gaps identified and addressed
- Resume tailored with AI support
- Job search tracking system in place
