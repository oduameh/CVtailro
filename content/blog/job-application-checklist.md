# Job Application Checklist: Resume, Cover Letter, and Follow-Up

Submitting a job application involves more than uploading a resume. A structured checklist ensures your CV is optimized, your cover letter is aligned, and your follow-up is scheduled. This guide provides a reusable checklist for every application.

## 1) Resume checklist

- Resume is tailored to the job description
- Top keywords appear in summary and skills
- Experience bullets include metrics
- Format is ATS-friendly

## 2) Cover letter checklist

- Role and company named in the first sentence
- Two achievements with measurable outcomes
- Keywords aligned with the job description

## 3) Application submission checklist

- Resume file name is clear and professional
- Correct file type (PDF or DOCX)
- All required fields completed

## 4) Follow-up checklist

- Follow-up scheduled for 5-7 business days
- Tracking spreadsheet updated

## 5) Final checklist

- Is the application complete and tailored?
- Are keywords aligned?
- Is follow-up scheduled?
