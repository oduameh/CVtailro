# AI + ATS Matching Explained: How Modern Screening Works

Modern hiring combines ATS keyword matching with AI-driven ranking. This means your resume must be both keyword-accurate and semantically rich. This guide explains how AI and ATS systems evaluate resumes and how to optimize for both.

## 1) ATS systems: keyword and structure

ATS systems parse your resume into sections, then compare keywords to the job description. Clean formatting and exact keyword matches are critical.

## 2) AI systems: semantic relevance

AI models evaluate meaning. They look for related terms, context, and outcomes. This is why semantic coverage and clear achievements matter.

## 3) How to optimize for both

- Use exact keywords in the skills section.
- Add semantic variants in experience bullets.
- Provide measurable outcomes to strengthen relevance.

## 4) Common AI/ATS pitfalls

- Overstuffing keywords without context
- Using complex layouts that break parsing
- Including vague achievements

## 5) Final checklist

- Keywords match the job description
- Resume is clean and readable
- Achievements include outcomes
