# Data Analyst Resume Guide: SQL, Dashboards, and ATS Keywords

Data analyst resumes should emphasize analytical impact, tools, and business outcomes. Recruiters want to see SQL, dashboarding, and measurable improvements in decision-making. This guide shows how to build a data analyst resume that performs in ATS systems.

## 1) Data analyst resume summary example

"Data analyst with 5 years of experience in SQL, Python, and dashboarding. Automated reporting to reduce manual effort by 35% and improved KPI visibility for marketing teams."

## 2) Analytics keywords to include

- SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI
- Data visualization, dashboards, KPI reporting
- A/B testing, cohort analysis, forecasting

## 3) Quantify analytics impact

Examples:

"Built a forecasting model that improved accuracy by 12%."

"Reduced reporting time by 30% by automating ETL workflows."

## 4) Tools and platforms

List tools clearly: SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau, Power BI, Looker.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are analytics tools listed?
- Are outcomes quantified?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
