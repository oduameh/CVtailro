# AI Resume Feedback Loop: Improve With Each Application

A resume improves fastest when you build a feedback loop. AI can help you analyze responses, identify gaps, and refine your resume over time. This guide explains a simple feedback loop you can use.

## 1) Capture feedback signals

Track:

- Applications with no response
- Interviews secured
- Offer outcomes

This data shows where your resume is strong or weak.

## 2) Use AI to analyze gaps

Prompt:

"Analyze these job descriptions and my resume to identify missing keywords and sections."

## 3) Iterate and test

Update your resume, then apply to new roles and track outcomes. This is an iterative improvement process.

## 4) Keep changes measurable

Only change one or two elements at a time so you can see what improves results.

## 5) Final checklist

- Feedback data is tracked
- Resume updated regularly
- AI outputs reviewed for accuracy
