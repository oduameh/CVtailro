# AI Interview Prep Coaching: Turn Your Resume Into Strong Answers

AI can help you transform resume bullets into interview-ready answers. This guide shows how to use AI to build STAR stories, practice answers, and improve confidence while keeping everything accurate.

## 1) Convert resume bullets into STAR stories

Prompt:

"Turn these resume bullets into STAR stories with clear metrics: [bullets]."

Review for accuracy, then practice out loud.

## 2) Build a question bank

Use AI to generate likely questions from the job description:

"List the top 10 interview questions for a [role] based on this job description."

## 3) Practice concise answers

AI can help shorten long answers. Prompt:

"Condense this response to 90 seconds while keeping key metrics."

## 4) Avoid AI pitfalls

- Do not memorize AI text word-for-word.
- Always ensure claims are true.

## 5) Final checklist

- 5-7 STAR stories prepared
- Metrics are accurate
- Answers align with resume keywords
