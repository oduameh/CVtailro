# AI Portfolio Builder: Turn Resume Projects Into Proof

AI tools can help you structure a portfolio faster, but the content must remain accurate and aligned with your resume. This guide shows how to build a portfolio that strengthens your CV and improves recruiter trust.

## 1) Select portfolio projects from your resume

Choose 2-5 projects that demonstrate measurable impact. Use AI to summarize each project consistently.

## 2) Use a simple project template

Template:

- Objective
- Tools
- Actions
- Outcome
- Metrics

AI can help turn raw notes into a concise summary.

## 3) Align portfolio keywords with your resume

Ensure the same keywords appear in both the resume and portfolio for consistency.

## 4) Avoid AI exaggeration

Never allow AI to invent outcomes or metrics. Portfolio content must be verifiable.

## 5) Final checklist

- Projects align with resume achievements
- Outcomes are quantified
- Keywords match the target role
