# Interview Prep Checklist Based on Your Resume

Your resume is the best starting point for interview preparation. Most interview questions are built around your experience bullets. This guide provides a checklist to turn your resume into strong interview answers.

## 1) Turn each resume bullet into a STAR story

For each key bullet, create a STAR answer:

- Situation
- Task
- Action
- Result

## 2) Highlight metrics

Make sure every story includes a measurable outcome. This shows credibility and impact.

## 3) Prepare for role-specific questions

Use the job description to predict interview topics. Match your resume keywords to likely questions.

## 4) Practice a 60-second summary

Prepare a concise "tell me about yourself" answer that matches your resume summary.

## 5) Final checklist

- Do you have 5-7 STAR stories ready?
- Are your metrics accurate and memorable?
- Can you explain every keyword on your resume?
