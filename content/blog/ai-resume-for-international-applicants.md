# AI Resume Tips for International Applicants

International job applications introduce extra complexity: different resume norms, language expectations, and ATS systems. AI can help you adapt quickly, but you must verify accuracy and local standards. This guide shows how to use AI for global resume adaptation.

## 1) Understand local resume norms

Some regions prefer CVs with more detail, while others expect short resumes. Use AI to summarize local expectations, then verify with reliable sources.

## 2) Translate and localize keywords

If applying in another language, use AI to translate keywords and job titles accurately. Keep the original terms where possible for ATS matching.

## 3) Adjust formatting for ATS

Regardless of location, ATS systems prefer clean, single-column layouts. Avoid complex templates.

## 4) Verify metrics and terminology

Ensure that AI translations do not change your metrics or role responsibilities.

## 5) Final checklist

- Resume aligns with local norms
- Keywords are translated accurately
- ATS-friendly formatting maintained
