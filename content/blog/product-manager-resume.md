# Product Manager Resume Guide: Roadmap Impact and ATS Keywords

Product manager resumes must show user impact, business outcomes, and cross-functional leadership. This guide shows how to build a product manager resume that aligns with ATS requirements and hiring manager expectations.

## 1) Product manager resume summary example

"Product manager with 6 years of experience in user research, roadmap strategy, and go-to-market execution. Launched products that improved activation by 18% and retention by 9%."

## 2) Product management keywords

- Roadmap, PRD, go-to-market
- User research, experimentation, analytics
- Stakeholder alignment, cross-functional leadership

## 3) Quantify product impact

Examples:

"Improved onboarding conversion from 32% to 41% through iterative UX testing."

"Reduced churn by 7% by launching a proactive retention feature."

## 4) Tools and platforms

List tools: Jira, Amplitude, Figma, SQL, Mixpanel.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are roadmap outcomes quantified?
- Are core PM keywords included?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
