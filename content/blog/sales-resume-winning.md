# Winning Sales Resume: Keywords, Quota, and Pipeline Metrics

Sales resumes are performance documents. Employers want to see quota attainment, pipeline metrics, and deal impact. This guide shows how to build a sales resume that highlights measurable outcomes and ATS keywords.

## 1) Sales resume summary example

"Sales manager with 9 years of experience in enterprise sales and pipeline management. Exceeded quota by 20% for three consecutive years and expanded key accounts by $1.8M ARR."

## 2) Sales keywords to include

- Quota attainment, pipeline management, CRM
- Prospecting, negotiation, deal velocity
- ARR growth, account expansion, retention

## 3) Quantify your sales impact

Use metrics like:

- Revenue closed
- Win rate improvements
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle reduction

Example bullet:

"Increased win rate from 22% to 31% by implementing a new discovery framework."

## 4) Tools and platforms

List CRMs and sales tools: Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Gong.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are revenue outcomes clear?
- Are CRM and sales tools listed?
- Does the resume align with the job description?
