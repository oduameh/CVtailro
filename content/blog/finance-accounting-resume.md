# Finance and Accounting Resume Guide: Metrics, Compliance, and ATS

Finance and accounting resumes should emphasize accuracy, compliance, and measurable business impact. Recruiters want to see forecasting, budgeting, and audit readiness. This guide shows how to build a finance resume that is ATS-friendly and outcome-focused.

## 1) Finance resume summary example

"Finance analyst with 6 years of experience in forecasting, budgeting, and variance analysis. Improved forecast accuracy to within 2% and reduced monthly close time by 3 days."

## 2) Finance keywords to include

- Forecasting, budgeting, variance analysis
- GAAP, audit, compliance
- Financial modeling, reporting, cost control

## 3) Quantify financial impact

Examples:

"Reduced operating costs by 12% through vendor renegotiation and expense analysis."

"Built a revenue forecast model that improved accuracy by 15%."

## 4) Highlight systems and tools

List relevant tools: Excel, SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, Tableau.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are compliance and audit terms included?
- Are financial outcomes quantified?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
