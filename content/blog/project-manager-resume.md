# Project Manager Resume Guide: Keywords, Delivery Metrics, and ATS

Project manager resumes should emphasize delivery, stakeholder alignment, and measurable outcomes. Recruiters want to see on-time delivery, budget management, and risk mitigation. This guide shows how to build a PM resume that performs in ATS systems and resonates with hiring managers.

## 1) Project manager resume summary example

"Project manager with 8 years of experience in Agile delivery and stakeholder management. Delivered a $2.4M platform migration on time and reduced project risk through structured governance."

## 2) Project management keywords

- Agile, Scrum, roadmap, stakeholder alignment
- Risk management, scope control, budget ownership
- Jira, Asana, MS Project

## 3) Quantify delivery impact

Examples:

"Delivered 12 cross-functional projects with a 95% on-time rate."

"Reduced project cycle time by 20% by implementing sprint retrospectives."

## 4) Certifications and frameworks

Include PMP, Scrum Master, or PRINCE2 if applicable.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are delivery metrics included?
- Are tools and frameworks listed?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
