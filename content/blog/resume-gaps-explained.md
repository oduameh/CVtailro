# How to Explain Employment Gaps on Your Resume

Employment gaps are common and do not have to hurt your job search. The key is to address them clearly and confidently while keeping your resume ATS-friendly. This guide shows how to explain gaps and keep your CV strong.

## 1) Be honest and concise

Do not hide gaps. Use clear dates and avoid misleading formatting. Honesty builds trust with recruiters.

## 2) Show productive use of time

If you took time off, highlight:

- Training or certifications
- Freelance projects
- Volunteering
- Caregiving responsibilities (if you want to share)

## 3) Use a resume summary to reframe

A strong summary can shift focus to your skills and current goals.

## 4) Address gaps in interviews

Prepare a short explanation that focuses on growth and readiness to return.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are dates clear and consistent?
- Is the gap explained through projects or training?
- Is the resume still keyword-optimized?
