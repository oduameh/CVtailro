# AI Resume Mistakes to Avoid: Accuracy, Ethics, and ATS

AI can improve your resume, but it can also create risks if used carelessly. This guide covers the most common AI resume mistakes and how to avoid them.

## 1) Fabricated achievements

Never include metrics or outcomes that did not happen. AI can suggest numbers, but you must verify them.

## 2) Keyword stuffing

Overloading your resume with keywords can hurt readability and credibility. Use keywords naturally.

## 3) Generic summaries

AI can generate generic summaries. Make sure your summary reflects your specific experience and target role.

## 4) Inconsistent dates or titles

AI rewrites can introduce inconsistencies. Always verify dates, titles, and company names.

## 5) Final checklist

- All claims are accurate
- Keywords are aligned and natural
- Resume remains ATS-friendly
