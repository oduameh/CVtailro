# AI-Optimized Resume That Still Reads Well for Humans

AI optimization is only useful if your resume still reads clearly to recruiters. This guide shows how to balance AI-driven keyword alignment with human readability.

## 1) Prioritize clarity over keyword density

Keywords should support your story, not overwhelm it. Use simple language and clear structure.

## 2) Keep bullets concise

Long bullets are harder to read. Aim for 1-2 lines with a clear outcome.

## 3) Use keywords where they make sense

Place exact keywords in skills and summaries. Use related terms in bullets.

## 4) Review for flow and logic

Read the resume as if you were a recruiter. Does it tell a coherent story? If not, revise.

## 5) Final checklist

- Resume is readable in under 30 seconds
- Keywords align with job description
- Metrics are clear and accurate
