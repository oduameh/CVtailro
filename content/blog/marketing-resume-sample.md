# Marketing Resume Sample: Keywords, Metrics, and ATS Strategy

A marketing resume should emphasize growth, conversion, and channel performance. Recruiters want to see clear metrics and evidence that you understand acquisition and retention. This guide provides a marketing resume structure and key keyword areas to improve ATS matching.

## 1) Marketing resume summary example

"Marketing manager with 7 years of experience in SEO, paid acquisition, and lifecycle marketing. Increased organic traffic by 42% and reduced CAC by 18% across B2B campaigns."

## 2) Marketing keywords to include

- SEO, content strategy, paid search
- Conversion rate optimization, attribution
- Email automation, lifecycle marketing
- CRM, analytics, A/B testing

## 3) Marketing achievement bullets

Use numbers:

"Launched a content strategy that generated 180K monthly organic visits and increased demo requests by 22%."

## 4) Portfolio and campaigns

Include notable campaigns, budgets, and measurable outcomes. Marketing resumes are stronger when they show campaign impact.

## 5) Final checklist

- Do you quantify campaign results?
- Are key tools and platforms listed?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
