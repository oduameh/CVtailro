# AI Networking Messages: Templates for LinkedIn and Email

Networking increases job search success, but writing outreach messages can be time-consuming. AI can help draft messages quickly, as long as you keep them specific and respectful. This guide provides templates and prompts.

## 1) LinkedIn connection request template

"Hi [Name], I noticed your work in [industry/team]. I am exploring [role] opportunities and would love to connect and learn about your experience. Thanks!"

## 2) Informational interview request

"Hi [Name], I am interested in [role/industry] and appreciated your recent post about [topic]. Would you be open to a 15-minute chat? I would value your perspective."

## 3) Follow-up after connecting

"Thanks for connecting, [Name]. If you have time in the next two weeks, I would love to ask a few questions about your role and career path."

## 4) AI prompt for personalization

"Personalize this networking message using the following profile details: [details]."

## 5) Final checklist

- Message is short and specific
- Clear reason for outreach
- Polite and professional tone
