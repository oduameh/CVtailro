# AI Career Coach Questions That Improve Your Resume

AI can act as a career coach if you ask the right questions. The goal is to surface real achievements, clarify your strengths, and improve your resume positioning. This guide provides practical AI career coach questions you can use.

## 1) Achievement discovery questions

- "What are my top 5 measurable achievements based on this resume?"
- "Which bullets show the most impact and why?"

## 2) Keyword alignment questions

- "Which keywords from this job description are missing in my resume?"
- "Where should I place these keywords for ATS alignment?"

## 3) Positioning questions

- "What is the best way to describe my career narrative in 3 sentences?"
- "Which role titles best match my experience?"

## 4) Skill gap questions

- "Which skills are missing for my target role?"
- "What projects could demonstrate these skills quickly?"

## 5) Final checklist

- Are responses grounded in real experience?
- Are suggested changes aligned with target roles?
- Are keywords included in summary and skills?
