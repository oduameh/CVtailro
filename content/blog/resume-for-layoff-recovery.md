# Resume After a Layoff: Rebuild Your Job Search Fast

A layoff can be challenging, but it does not define your career. A strong resume helps you restart quickly and confidently. This guide shows how to update your CV after a layoff and improve job application results.

## 1) Reframe your resume summary

Focus on your skills and achievements, not the layoff. Use a summary that highlights your value and direction.

## 2) Highlight measurable impact

Use your best achievements to show results. This shifts attention to your contributions.

## 3) Address gaps if needed

If there is a gap, keep dates clear and consider adding a short line about training or projects.

## 4) Use a structured job search plan

Combine resume updates with a consistent application and follow-up routine.

## 5) Final checklist

- Is your summary strong and forward-looking?
- Are your top achievements visible?
- Are keywords aligned with the target roles?
