# Resume Length: One Page or Two in 2026?

One of the most common resume questions is whether to use one page or two. The answer depends on your experience level and the role. This guide explains how to choose the right resume length without hurting ATS performance.

## 1) One page is best for early career

If you have less than 5 years of experience, a one-page resume is often ideal. It forces focus on the most relevant content.

## 2) Two pages are acceptable for experienced professionals

If you have 8+ years of relevant experience, two pages can be appropriate, especially for technical or leadership roles.

## 3) Prioritize relevance over length

It is better to have one strong page than two pages of filler. Every bullet should be relevant to the role.

## 4) ATS considerations

ATS systems handle one or two pages equally, but they prioritize keyword relevance. Do not add extra pages without relevant content.

## 5) Final checklist

- Does every section add value?
- Is the resume easy to scan?
- Are keywords aligned with the job description?
