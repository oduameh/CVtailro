# Changing Industries: How to Show Transferable Skills on Your Resume

Changing industries is possible when you translate your experience into the language of the new role. This guide shows how to highlight transferable skills, align keywords, and build a resume that signals fit.

## 1) Identify transferable skills

Common transferable skills include:

- Project management
- Stakeholder communication
- Data analysis
- Process improvement
- Customer experience

Use the job description to decide which skills matter most.

## 2) Translate achievements into new industry language

If the job requires "risk management," highlight the risk-related parts of your previous work. Use the exact wording from the posting.

## 3) Add relevant projects or training

Courses, certifications, or side projects can demonstrate commitment to the new industry.

## 4) Use a career-change summary

A strong summary can connect your past experience to the new role using keywords.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are keywords aligned with the new industry?
- Are transferable skills visible?
- Does the resume show outcomes?
