# Customer Success Resume Guide: Retention Metrics and ATS Keywords

Customer success resumes should emphasize retention, onboarding, and account growth. Recruiters want to see how you reduced churn, improved NPS, and expanded revenue. This guide shows how to build a CSM resume that performs in ATS systems.

## 1) Customer success resume summary example

"Customer success manager with 6 years of experience in onboarding and retention. Reduced churn by 9% and improved NPS from 42 to 57 across a 120-account portfolio."

## 2) Customer success keywords

- Onboarding, retention, churn reduction
- Customer lifecycle management, CRM
- NPS, CSAT, account expansion

## 3) Quantify customer impact

Examples:

"Improved onboarding completion rate from 68% to 82%."

"Expanded 25 accounts, generating $1.2M in expansion ARR."

## 4) Tools and platforms

List tools: Salesforce, Gainsight, HubSpot, Zendesk.

## 5) Final checklist

- Are retention metrics included?
- Are customer success keywords aligned?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
