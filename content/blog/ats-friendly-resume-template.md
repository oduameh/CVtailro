# ATS-Friendly Resume Template You Can Use in 2026

An ATS-friendly resume template keeps your content readable for both software and recruiters. This guide provides a simple template and explains how to customize it for any role.

## 1) ATS-friendly resume template structure

Use this structure:

1. Name and contact info
2. Resume summary
3. Skills
4. Experience
5. Education
6. Certifications

This order aligns with ATS parsing and recruiter scanning habits.

## 2) Template example (text layout)

Name  
City, State | Email | Phone | LinkedIn

Summary  
3-5 lines with keywords and metrics

Skills  
List of tools and role-specific skills

Experience  
Role, Company, Dates  
- Bullet with outcome and metric  
- Bullet with tool and result

Education  
Degree, School, Year

Certifications  
Certification, Issuer, Year

## 3) Customization tips

- Tailor the summary to the job description.
- Update skills for each role.
- Emphasize relevant achievements in top bullets.

## 4) Final checklist

- Is the template one column?
- Are headings standard and consistent?
- Are keywords aligned with the job description?
