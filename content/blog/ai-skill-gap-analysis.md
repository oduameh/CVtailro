# AI Skill Gap Analysis for Career Growth

AI can compare your resume to job descriptions and highlight missing skills. This helps you build a targeted learning plan and accelerate career growth. This guide shows a simple skill gap analysis workflow.

## 1) Gather target job descriptions

Collect 5-10 job descriptions for your target role. Use AI to extract common skills and requirements.

## 2) Compare against your resume

Prompt:

"Compare this resume to these job descriptions and list missing skills."

## 3) Prioritize gaps

Focus on the top 5-7 missing skills that appear in most postings. These are high-leverage gaps.

## 4) Build a learning plan

For each gap, choose a course, project, or certification. Track progress weekly.

## 5) Update your resume as you learn

Add new skills and projects to your resume to improve ATS matching.
