# Resume Proofreading Guide: Catch Errors Before You Apply

Even small resume errors can reduce interview chances. Proofreading ensures your CV is accurate, consistent, and ATS-friendly. This guide provides a structured proofreading routine.

## 1) Check for formatting consistency

- Fonts, sizes, and spacing are consistent
- Headings are uniform
- Dates follow the same format

## 2) Verify contact information

Make sure email, phone, and LinkedIn links are correct.

## 3) Review for keyword alignment

Confirm that top keywords from the job description appear in your summary, skills, and experience sections.

## 4) Read out loud

Reading out loud helps catch awkward phrasing and missing words.

## 5) Final checklist

- No typos or grammar errors
- Metrics are accurate
- ATS-friendly format
