# AI Hiring Bias and How to Mitigate It in Your Resume

AI screening can introduce bias if models rely on patterns that reflect historical data. While you cannot control the model, you can structure your resume to reduce ambiguity and emphasize objective evidence. This guide explains practical steps.

## 1) Use clear, measurable achievements

Metrics reduce ambiguity and help AI systems focus on outcomes rather than subjective interpretation.

## 2) Avoid ambiguous job titles

If your title was non-standard, include a clarifying subtitle:

"Customer Experience Lead (Customer Success Manager equivalent)"

## 3) Focus on role-relevant keywords

This increases relevance and reduces the risk of misclassification.

## 4) Keep formatting simple

Complex formatting can reduce parsing accuracy, which can amplify bias.

## 5) Final checklist

- Achievements are measurable
- Titles are clear
- Keywords align with role requirements
