# AI Resume Keyword Expansion: Improve Semantic Coverage

AI systems evaluate meaning, not just exact keywords. Keyword expansion helps you capture semantic coverage while still meeting ATS requirements. This guide shows how to expand resume keywords responsibly.

## 1) Extract core keywords

Start with the job description and list the top required keywords.

## 2) Expand with related terms

Use AI to generate related terms:

"Generate related skills and synonyms for these keywords: [list]."

Example: "data visualization" -> dashboards, reporting, BI, storytelling.

## 3) Place expansions naturally

Include expanded terms in experience bullets and project descriptions. Keep exact keywords in the skills section.

## 4) Avoid keyword dilution

Do not add unrelated terms just to appear broad. Every keyword should be tied to real experience.

## 5) Final checklist

- Exact keywords included
- Semantic variants added naturally
- Resume remains accurate
