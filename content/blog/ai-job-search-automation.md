# AI Job Search Automation: Tools, Workflows, and Limits

AI can automate repetitive parts of job searching, but quality still depends on your oversight. This guide explains where AI helps most, how to build workflows, and where to stay manual.

## 1) What AI can automate

- Keyword extraction from job descriptions
- Resume tailoring suggestions
- Drafting cover letters and follow-ups
- Tracking applications and reminders

## 2) What should remain manual

- Final resume review
- Accuracy checks for metrics
- Decisions about role fit

## 3) A simple automation workflow

1. Use AI to extract keywords from a job description.
2. Update resume summary and skills with those keywords.
3. Draft a short cover letter using AI, then edit manually.
4. Use a tracker to schedule follow-ups.

## 4) Common automation mistakes

- Submitting AI drafts without review
- Over-applying to roles without fit
- Using generic AI prompts

## 5) Final checklist

- Is the resume still accurate?
- Are AI outputs reviewed by a human?
- Are job applications targeted?
