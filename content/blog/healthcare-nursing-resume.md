# Healthcare and Nursing Resume Guide: ATS-Friendly CV for Clinical Roles

Healthcare resumes must show clinical competence, compliance, and patient outcomes. Employers want to see evidence of quality care, safety practices, and teamwork. This guide shows how to build a healthcare or nursing resume that performs well in ATS systems.

## 1) Nursing resume summary example

"Registered nurse with 7 years of experience in acute care and patient education. Improved patient satisfaction scores by 11% and reduced readmissions through care coordination."

## 2) Healthcare keywords to include

- Patient care, clinical documentation
- Compliance, HIPAA, safety protocols
- Care coordination, triage, EHR systems

## 3) Quantify patient outcomes

Example:

"Reduced medication errors by 18% through improved documentation and protocol training."

## 4) Highlight certifications

Include credentials and certifications prominently (RN, BSN, ACLS, BLS).

## 5) Final checklist

- Are compliance and safety terms included?
- Are patient outcomes quantified?
- Is the resume ATS-friendly?
